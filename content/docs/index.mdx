Welcome to the WAVS Docs!

[WAVS](https://github.com/Lay3rLabs/WAVS) (Web Assembly Verifiable Services) is the event-driven framework for distributed architectures, allowing you to ship verifiable applications faster with our all-in-one framework. Use this documentation to learn [about WAVS](/overview), [how it works](/how-it-works), and how to [start building your verifiable service](./tutorial/1-overview).
